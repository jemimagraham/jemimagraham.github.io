About me

So far, I have found the course very enjoyable and have found the weekly assessed problems fascinating. I have also enjoyed the computing labs where I was first introduced to the coding language python. In these labs, I was set the task of plotting the trajectories and observing the behaviour of spacecraft around Mars and the Comet 67P. I would say that it was this project that gave me the 'coding-bug', especially as I obtained a first.
